FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1.Q: How much does Ember charge for crowdfunding and investing

A: 0%. Ember Launchpad do not charge fees for any of the crowdfunding projects or investments. However, 5% of the total amount raised will be paid towards content creators and influencers that can help to promote your campaign. Ember do charge a fee of 20% for digital distribution.

2. Q: What happened if I did not raise enough to successfully crowdfund the project?

A: All the money will be refunded back to the supports.

3. Q: Can someone support the project and invest at the same time?

A: Yes by all means.

4. Q: What are the criteria for me to start a crowdfunding campaign?

A: We evaluate the projects submitted by a few elements such as ability to work under a budget and timeline, experience and track record of the development team, creativity, as well as potential market of the game.

5. Q: I already have a working demo and playable product, can I still start an investment campaign?

A: Yes but on a per case basis. We will have to evaluate the elements mentioned above.

6. Q: Am I obligated to publish the game on Ember?

A: No, developers can feel free to publish the project anywhere you like, however publishing the game on our platform is strongly encouraged as it is our only source of income. You also get to enjoy benefits such as higher revenue split. If you would like to publish in China, Ember do provide services on publishing and licensing as well. You may contact us to find out more.

7.Q: Is there any restriction towards the platform of the game? Eg mobile or console games

A: No, however Ember will not be able to subsidise the 8% revenue split towards investors from any platform other than PC.

8.Q: What are the materials I need to submit?

A: The development team have to be registered companies, providing us the Company information such as Company name, company registration number, shareholder information, along with shareholder’s particulars such as name, identification, residential address etc. Experience and tract record of the team will be very important when it comes to our evaluation as well. Regarding the projects, please provide as much information as possible, such as concept arts, design materials, world setting and concepts etc. Developers are strongly encouraged to prepare a trailer too.

9.Q: Who owns the IP?

A: Developers will have full IP control.

10.Q: What are things considered as my game’s revenue?

A: This includes game sales, micro transactions, Downloadable contents, subscriptions ( battle pass etc). About us Ember is an AI driven digital distribution platform based in Singapore. We bring quality, immersive games to the gaming community by empowering independent Asian studios in the development and bring them to the global market. From crowdfunding to campaign optimization, we enable studios to launch games successfully by making it not only more cost efficient, but also simpler.
