welcome to coliving App
To make your life in a community as easy as possible

Are you moving to a new city to participate in an exchange, to do an internship or simply just to start a new work? Are you feeling anxious and insecure about living abroad, or being far away from home and your habitual environment and finding your place in a new and international Community?

Are you facing problems with finding decent and proper flatmates or managing finances and everyday house chores in your apartment? All of the above mentioned are important points we‘d like to cover. We are providing you with all sorts of solutions with our ColivingApp. Download it for free and enjoy an easier life in your community.

Our main goal from ColivingApp is to take those insecurities and fears away and make your life in a new community as easy as possible.

What is coliving

Coliving is a modern form of housing where residents do not only share their living space but also a set of interests, values, and/or intentions. It's a new take on an old idea, elaborated by a millennial generation that values things like openness and collaboration, social networking, and the sharing economy. Meet people from all around the world and let yourself go with the flow.

Helping to find new flatmates

  • Changing the city and therefore changing your place of residence and environment is often one of the biggest challenges and reason for doubts and insecurities. In fact, this might even be the biggest fear when you’re moving to a new city, not to mention if you’re even going abroad.

  • Get to know your future flatmates using the app, even before moving into the city. Details about your preferential location to live, the price range you are willing to pay, personal habits or interests, the languages being spoken, and many more details will help you to find the perfect place to live. Personalise your account by adding your individual hobbies and habits and get your new flatmates to know you before living together. Because coliving is about people.

Splitting the bills

  • We are aware of the potential problems that can occur in terms of keeping the financial expenses between you and your flatmates under control. For instance, someone buys detergents for 5EUR once and trying to remember mind whose turn it is to buy detergents the next time, or generally splitting the costs between you and your flatmates immediately and expecting everyone to pay the exact amount of 1.66EUR?

  • How about an app, that is doing all the work for you. You can easily just upload an amount of money, and the app will divide any expenses that have been made and also summarized all sorts of expenses within the apartment’s finances? No more problems with keeping track and writing down every little detail or trying to remind oblivious flatmates.

Communication

  • We know that communication is the key, especially in shared flats. Failed communication through several instant messengers such as such Whatsapp or Messenger or even email can be extremely annoying and nerve-racking.

  • How about a solution that is enabling you to communicate with your landlord and your flatmates directly?

House chores management

  • Distribution of everyday chores in a household, such as cleaning the living room, dusting or vacuuming, when living with other people, is an exemplary and recurring problem in shared flats.

  • Set up a house chores list with your flatmates with notifications coming directly to your phone.

Landlords - Properties Management

Coliving app does not only support tenants but also landlords who rent their apartments. Connect with your tenants and be able to check their info right away. Manage the tenants moving in and moving out. Use our messenger to communicate with your tenants and also get to know about any issues that can be shared with you through the app immediately. Everything for free.


The same features as on the website and in the app will help you to manage your premises even better. Spare yourself dealing with bothersome and time-consuming apartment managements in a tedious way by using our app.
